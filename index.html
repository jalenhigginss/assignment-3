introduction

Welcome to a quick, student-friendly guide to eating within walking distance of the Petty building at UNCG. This page highlights three nearby spots where you can grab coffee, sushi, hot dogs, and ice cream without a car. Each section includes a short overview of the restaurant and three specific dishes with captions so you can decide fast between classes.

three nearby spots

tate street coffee house

The best coffee shop on Tate Street. Known for reliable espresso drinks, comfy seating, and local art. Hours vary slightly by day, mornings to late evenings.

extras

Keep an eye on daily specials and lunch combos, prices shift and student-friendly deals pop up. Some campus-run spots take flex dollars, but most Tate Street staples do not, so plan for card/cash just in case.
